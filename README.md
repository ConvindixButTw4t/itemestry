
Itemestry
=======

A mod, that adds new **ingots**, **dusts**, **plates** and **fluids** 

List of all items
==========
1. **Ores**
   - Tin ore
   - Tungsten ore 
   - Titanium ore 
   - Bluestone ore
   

2. **Ingots**
   - Tungsten ingot 
   - Bronze ingot  
   - Niobium ingot 
   - Iridium ingot 
   - Titanium ingot 
   - Obsidian ingot 
   - Blue iron ingot 
   - Red iron ingot 
   - Steel ingot 


3. Dusts
   - Copper dust  
   - Iron dust 
   - Tin dust 
   - Bronze dust  
   - Diamond dust  
   - Titanium dust 
   - Tungsten dust 
   - Niobium dust 
   - Bluestone dust 
   - Obsidian dust 
   - Steel dust 


4. Plates
   - Copper plate 
   - Iron plate 
   - Tin plate 
   - Bronze plate  
   - Titanium plate 
   - Neutronium plate 
   - Tungsten plate 
   - Niobium plate
   - Steel plate 
   - Obsidian plate 

All items, except *Tungsten*, *Tin*, *Titanium* ingots and *Bluestone* *dust*, **DON'T** have crafts, you must add it by yourself
